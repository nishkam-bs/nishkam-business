Schedule

Two-year assessment programme.

The complete assessment schedule mapped week by week from Year 10 Day 1 to the final exam. Every week has a focus topic, quiz code and past paper question.

How to use this schedule: Each week lists the topic being taught, a quiz code for retrieval practice, and a past paper question for homework or in-class practice. Use this alongside the unit pages and revision guide.
How this works: Each week you will complete two tasks. (1) A knowledge quiz on the topic being taught that week — enter the quiz code on the Quiz page. (2) A past paper question submitted to the AI Marker — practising real AQA exam technique from the start. As the course progresses, you will also see CUMULATIVE review questions testing topics from previous units.
