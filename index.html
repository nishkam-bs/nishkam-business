Our Three Curriculum Pillars

Faith-Inspired

Learning from the wisdom of all traditions. Businesses that act with integrity and compassion reflect values at the heart of every great faith.

Virtues-Led

Fostering compassion, humility, justice and courage. Every business decision is an ethical choice — a chance to practise virtue.

Aspiring for Excellence

Becoming the best human beings we can possibly be. In Business Studies, this means thinking critically, writing with precision, and achieving Grade 9.

Peacefulness · Diligence · Determination

Peacefulness
In Business Studies
Approach complex questions with a calm, focused mind. Listen to different stakeholder perspectives with respect. Create a learning environment where every voice is valued.
Diligence
In Business Studies
Commit fully to mastering each topic. Check your chains of analysis, refine your evaluations, and never submit work that is less than your best.
Determination
In Business Studies
Persevere through challenging 9 and 12-mark questions. Treat every mistake as a stepping stone. Keep pushing toward your target grade and beyond.
