Process

Three steps to better answers.

Select a past paper question or enter your own. Submit your answer. Receive detailed feedback in seconds.

1.

Select & submit

Enter your name. Pick a question from the 2019–2025 past paper bank, or paste a custom question. Type your answer or upload a photo of handwritten work.

2.

Examiner analysis

Your answer is analysed against AQA mark scheme criteria — checking chains of analysis, data manipulation, case study application and evaluation quality.

3.

Detailed feedback
